Priorities

Shweta's vision for Cedar Park's future

As a member of the Cedar Park City Council, I will focus on these key priorities to ensure our community continues to thrive:

Safe Neighborhoods

Support and strengthen our Police, Fire, and First responder services to keep Cedar Park safe, responsive, and prepared.

Strong Public Schools

Strong schools are the foundation of a strong community. I am committed to ensuring Cedar Park remains a great place to raise a family by supporting educational excellence.

Supporting Local Businesses

Encourage residents to shop local and support small businesses, keeping revenue within our community and strengthening our local economy.
#staylocal #shoplocal

Smart Infrastructure & Mobility

As Cedar Park grows, smart infrastructure— expanded roads, on-demand transit, and safe bike and walk paths—will ease traffic and support workforce housing. Smart zoning and partnerships will help teachers, first responders, seniors, and young families live here while preserving neighborhood character.

Sustainable Economic Development

Promote responsible, long-term growth that benefits all residents while preserving our quality of life. I will work to attract businesses that align with our community values and create opportunities for local entrepreneurs.

Shared Values. Stronger Community.

Cedar Park is built on Texas values—family, service, and personal responsibility—and preserving them means protecting our city's character while guiding thoughtful growth. I am committed to listening, engaging every resident, and ensuring all voices help shape a connected, inclusive Cedar Park that works for everyone.

Join Us in Building Cedar Park's Future

These priorities reflect my commitment to preserving what makes Cedar Park special while preparing for responsible growth. If you share these values, I'd love to have your support.
